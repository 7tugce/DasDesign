OUR DESIGN PROCESS

1 - MEETING

The first step in the process is to schedule a meeting at our office to learn about your expectations and preferences. We then move on to budgeting, where we create a general vision for the project and inform you about the process. This stage usually takes about an hour and we'll schedule an appointment for impression-taking.

2-SURVEY

During the survey, the building's general details are carefully noted, including its layout, dimensions, and any unique features or areas that require special consideration.
This information is then used to create detailed plans and designs that take into account the building's structure and functionality.

3- SETTLEMENT PLAN

We create 2D layout alternatives for ergonomic design with criteria such as passage distances, space for each unit, and optimum usage standards.

We critique the advantages of each alternative together with you and submit it for your approval, online or face-to-face.

4- 3D DESIGN

The 3D stage we made on your approved plan we designed with aesthetics to tell your style in the best way to reflect you most accurately. This stage is where we make your expectations applicable to your dreams and picture of your home you can see, and we provide the opportunity to walk around the areas.

5-REVISED

If there are any details you want to change at this stage, revisions makes.

In furniture details, without changing the residential area, the revisions you want in color and material selections are reflected in the drawing and all selections are finalized.

6-FINAL

You approve the final design, and the dates are ready for production and delivery. Agreed conditions made into a form contract signed by the parties, including details such as material, color and brand with the addition of 3D visuals. And the production and application process starts.

OUR PRODUCTION PROCESS

1-PLANNING

Team leaders of all services to be provided, business and personnel planning is done, material lists are created and deadlines determines.
Products are ordered and will be ready for application is drawn into stock.

2-PRODUCTION

The production of the products begins. Ordered lighting, decorative objects, and carpets are taken into stock. Application areas necessary for infrastructure preparations, renovation, repair and construction works are ready for assembly.

3-IMPLEMENTATION

Installation of all units and lighting products, such as the TV unit, kitchen, bathroom, bed and dressing rooms, specially produced for your area makes. Your furniture and decorative products are placed.

4- DELIVERY

Controls of products and applications are made, and any corrections and settings are provided if necessary. After a small introduction, including usage recommendations, the house was delivered to its owner with fine cleaning.
